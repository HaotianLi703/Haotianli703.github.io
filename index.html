About Me

I am a PhD Student in Operations Research at the Department of Industrial Engineering & Decision Analytics, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). My research currently focuses on healthcare management, where I use stochastic modeling, game theory and machine learning to improve the utilization efficiency of medical resources.

My research agenda is organized around two primary dimensions:

• Disease Management: Designing efficient tools for disease detection and prevention.

• Healthcare Operations: Optimizing the performance of service components within healthcare systems, with a focus on both incentive-level and operational-level interventions.
