Welcom to my website! 

My name is **Haotian Li/李浩天**, and I am currently pursuing a master’s degree in Management Science under the supervision of <a href="https://www.acem.sjtu.edu.cn/en/faculty/wanguohua.html">Professor Guohua Wan</a> at the <a href="https://www.acem.sjtu.edu.cn/index.html">Antai College of Economics and Management, Shanghai Jiao Tong University</a>/上海交通大学安泰经管学院. Prior to this, I obtained a bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering and Automation from <a href="https://seea.tju.edu.cn/">Tianjin University</a>. I am currently working on my PhD application in Operations Management/Industrial Engineering

My current research interests focus on data-driven stochastic decision making, particularly in the field of healthcare management.
